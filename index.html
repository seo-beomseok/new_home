About Me

I am an Economist and Data scientist in Bank of Korea. I recieved my Ph.D. in Statistics at Pennsylvania State University under Dr. Jia Li and Dr. Lynn Lin about Interpretable Statistical Learning.

I am currently working on various text-related economic and financial problems and machine learning modeling for economic data.

Research Interests

My research interests include a range of related problems in:

  • Interpretable neural networks and machine learning,
  • Human-machine interaction for social science and finance,
  • Text-related statistical modeling, High-dimensional data mining, Nonparametric methods.
